\noindent A few concepts that are confusing but important to understand:\\\\
1. $s_{i}$ strictly dominates $s_{i}'$.\\
$s_{i}'$ is strictly dominated by $s_{i}$.
\vspace*{6\baselineskip}

\noindent2. $s_{i}$ weakly dominates $s_{i}'$.
$s_{i}'$ is weakly dominated by $s_{i}$.
\vspace*{6\baselineskip}

\noindent 3. $s_{i}$ is a strictly dominant strategy.
\vspace*{6\baselineskip}

\noindent 4. $s_{i}$ is a weakly dominant strategy.
\vspace*{6\baselineskip}

\newpage
\noindent
5. $s^{*}$ is a strictly dominant strategy equilibrium.
\vspace*{6\baselineskip}

\noindent
6. $s^{*}$ is a weakly dominant strategy equilibrium.
\vspace*{6\baselineskip}

\noindent
7. A game is (strict) dominance solvable.
\vspace*{6\baselineskip}

\noindent
8. A game is weak dominance solvable.
